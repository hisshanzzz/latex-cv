How SpellQuest works

  1. Pick your track: Grade 1–5, 6–9, 10–11, 12–13, or Students.
  2. Choose Classic Bee (hear & spell) or Letter Hive (build words from 7 letters).
  3. Climb 6 tiers × 5 stages. Earn streaks, use limited hints, and unlock the next stage.
  4. Browser voice reads each bee word — turn up your volume.
